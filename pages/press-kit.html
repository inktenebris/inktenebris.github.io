Artist Overview

INKTENEBRIS is the artistic practice of Soumalya Sarkar, an independent dark surreal artist working primarily with ballpoint pen. The work explores psychological horror, identity, transformation, memory and the subconscious through monochromatic imagery constructed from thousands of layered marks. Each artwork exists between familiarity and distortion, inviting personal interpretation while challenging conventional visual comfort.

Artist Statement

INKTENEBRIS is an ongoing exploration of darkness, identity, memory and transformation. Working exclusively with ballpoint pen, I create surreal monochromatic drawings that emerge through thousands of deliberate marks and layers of ink.Rather than presenting direct answers, these works invite interpretation and allow viewers to discover their own meanings within the imagery. Through INKTENEBRIS, I seek to create artwork that lingers beyond the moment it is seen.
