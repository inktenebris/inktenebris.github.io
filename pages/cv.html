Artist Profile

Soumalya Sarkar is an independent dark surreal artist working primarily with ballpoint pen. Through INKTENEBRIS, he explores themes of identity, transformation, memory, psychological horror, isolation and subconscious imagery. His work combines meticulous mark-making with symbolic visual narratives that exist between dreamlike experiences and unsettling realities.

Education

Self-directed study in drawing, graphic design, visual storytelling and digital media.
