INKTENEBRIS

DOWNLOAD ARTIST STATEMENT

INKTENEBRIS is an ongoing exploration of darkness, identity, memory and transformation. Working exclusively with ballpoint pen, I create surreal monochromatic drawings that emerge through thousands of deliberate marks and layers of ink. My work is influenced by psychological horror, dream imagery, isolation and the hidden structures of the subconscious. Human forms dissolve, familiar objects become unsettling, and fragments of memory are reconstructed into visual narratives. Rather than presenting direct answers, these drawings invite interpretation. They exist in the space between beauty and discomfort, familiarity and distortion, reality and imagination. The slow and demanding nature of ballpoint pen drawing is central to the process. Every mark is permanent. Every layer contributes to an image that gradually emerges from darkness. Through INKTENEBRIS, I seek to create artwork that lingers beyond the moment it is seen—images that provoke curiosity, unease and reflection while remaining open to personal meaning.
